Creating a Botnet: Trojans

A Trojan is a type of malicious software that disguises itself as a legitimate program and tricks users into downloading and running it, allowing hackers to gain unauthorized access to their system.

Here are some ways trojans can be downloaded:

  • social engineering
  • software downloads
  • email attachments

Once this malware is unknowingly downloaded onto your computer, it can join a network of other infected devices and be used to disrupt the network of a targeted site, simply by visiting that site along with all the other infected. Click on the trojan horse to learn more!
