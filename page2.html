DDoS: Denial-of-Service

Denial-of-Service attacks, mostly known as a DDoS attacks, are malicious attempts to disrupt the normal traffic of a targeted server, service or network by overwhelming the target or its surrounding infrastructure with a flood of Internet traffic. Have you noticed any signs of >

  • Unusually slow network performance.
  • The inability to access any website.
  • An IP address makes an unusually large number of requests in a limited timespan.
